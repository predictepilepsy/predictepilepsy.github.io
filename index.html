Withdrawal of Antiseizure Medication after epilepsy Surgery (WAMS)

This clinical tool enables individualised risk assessment of postsurgical anti-seizure medication (ASM) withdrawal. It is meant to be used only by medical professionals to improve prediction of seizure outcome after ASM withdrawal following epilepsy surgery.
